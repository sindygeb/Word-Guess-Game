Super Smash Bros Hangman!

Can you guess which Nintendo character is displayed in the silhouette below?

How to Play:

  1. Guess a letter by pressing a key on your keyboard.
  2. If your letter shows up - HEY! - You're one step closer to solving who it is!
  3. If your letter does NOT show up - BUMMER - but keep on guessing!
  4. You have 10 guesses to try and fill the blank.
  5. There are 15 characters to guess total! BUT be warned - once you lose, the game is over.
  6. If you get ALL 15, then YOU WIN!

Good Luck!
